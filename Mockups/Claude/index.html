Study Debrief

This experiment investigated the halo effect with AI-generated virtual instructors.

What We Were Testing:

You were randomly assigned to watch one of two versions of the same AI instructor:

  • Warm condition: Friendly, enthusiastic demeanor
  • Cold condition: Neutral, distant demeanor

You watched the version.

The Halo Effect:

Research shows that our overall impression of someone (whether we like them) can unconsciously influence how we perceive their specific traits‚Äîeven objective ones like appearance or voice quality.

This study extends the classic 1977 research by Nisbett & Wilson to AI-generated agents, exploring whether humans exhibit similar biases toward virtual instructors.
